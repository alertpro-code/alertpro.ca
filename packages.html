How Our Pricing Works

Here's exactly how it works.

Every package starts with the same foundation: a Qolsys IQ4 smart panel, 2 door/window contacts, and a motion sensor — all included, all installed by our technician for a flat $199. The monthly fee is what changes between packages, because higher tiers include more hardware. Think of it like a phone plan — the device is built into the cost, and we're not pretending otherwise. What you won't get from us is a surprise invoice, a hidden activation fee, or a pushy upsell call. And if you ever want to add more — an extra sensor, a camera, a doorbell — just call us. We'll come back and add it. No obligation to change your plan.
