Home security in Whitby

Whitby is one of Ontario's fastest-growing cities, with new subdivisions expanding north and a dense, established core along the lakeshore. Break-in patterns across Durham Region tend to cluster around transitional periods — new developments, high turnover, and opportunistic entries through side doors and garage access points that homeowners underestimate.

AlertPRO serves all Whitby neighbourhoods, including Pringle Creek, Rolling Acres, Brooklin, Williamsburg, downtown Whitby, and the communities along the waterfront. Most installations in Whitby are completed within the same week you reach out.

Whitby homeowners with a professionally monitored alarm system backed by ULC-listed monitoring typically qualify for a home insurance discount of 5–20% with Ontario insurers. On an average policy, that's $40–$80 per month in savings — which often offsets part or all of the monitoring fee. It's worth calling your insurer before you decide.
