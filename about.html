Who We Are

A Durham Region company, through and through.

AlertPRO was founded and is headquartered in Whitby, Ontario. We started because we saw a gap in the market: large national security companies were charging premium prices while delivering impersonal service through contractors and overseas monitoring centres.

We do things differently. Our technicians are local employees — not subcontractors. They live in the same communities they protect. When you call us, you speak with someone who knows your neighbourhood, not someone reading from a script.

We use the Qolsys IQ4 — one of the most advanced smart security panels available — and monitor through a fully ULC-listed Canadian monitoring centre. You get enterprise-grade technology with small-business care.
