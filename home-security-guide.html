How does monitored home security work?

When your alarm triggers, the signal is sent instantly to a 24/7 ULC-listed monitoring centre. An alarm responder assesses the situation and coordinates next steps — including dispatching police, fire, or medical services when needed. AlertPRO monitoring operates around the clock, every day of the year.
